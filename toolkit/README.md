Welcome to the **Enterprise IT Governance & Automation Toolkit**. This repository of industry-standard templates and data-driven trackers is now fully upgraded to comply with **ITIL 4** and **ISO/IEC 20000** standards.

These tools bridge the gap between traditional IT Service Delivery and modern DevOps/SRE automation, providing a robust governance framework for operational excellence.

## 🏗️ Enterprise Compliance Features
All templates in this toolkit now include:
- **Document Control & Governance:** Versioning, ownership, and review cycles.
- **Traceability Matrix:** Direct linkage between Incidents, Problems, Changes, RCAs, and CAPAs.
- **RACI Accountability:** Clear definition of Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed roles.
- **Audit Evidence:** Dedicated sections for logs, screenshots, and monitoring alerts.
- **Tool-Ingestion Readiness:** Structured table formats compatible with Jira, ServiceNow, and Azure DevOps.
