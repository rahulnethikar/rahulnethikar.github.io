# IT Governance & Automation Toolkit

Welcome to the **IT Governance & Automation Toolkit**. This repository of industry-standard templates and data-driven trackers is designed to bridge the gap between traditional IT Service Delivery and modern DevOps/SRE automation.

Whether you are managing a global service desk or engineering a self-healing cloud infrastructure, these tools provide the structural foundation for operational excellence.

---

## 🛠️ Strategic Governance
*Standardizing executive decision-making and vendor management.*

| Tool | When to Use |
| :--- | :--- |
| [Business Case](./Business-Case.md) | Propose new initiatives or capital expenditures for executive approval. |
| [Request for Proposal (RFP)](./RFP.md) | Standardize vendor selection and competitive bidding processes. |
