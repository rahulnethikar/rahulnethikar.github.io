## ERROR CLASSIFICATION & NPS IMPACT MODEL

| Error Category | Specific Error | Potential Detractor Trigger | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **1. Process Error** | Skipped IVR validation check | Asking same info twice (Frustration) | High |
| **2. Technical Error** | Incorrect DSL profile sync | Problem not resolved (Repeat Call) | Medium |
| **3. Voice & Accent** | Fast Rate of Speech | Misunderstanding (Clarification loops) | Low |
| **4. Behavioral** | Lack of Empathy | Customer feels unheard (NPS Detractor) | High |
| **5. Knowledge Gap** | Unaware of new firmware update | Incorrect resolution (FCR failure) | Medium |
| **6. Tenurity Impact** | Rushing through closure | "Just another number" (Poor perception) | Medium |

## IMPACT MAPPING (The "Detractor Trigger" Matrix)

| Error Type | NPS Impact | Recovery Action | Owner |
| :--- | :---: | :--- | :--- |
| **Empathy Gap** | -40 Points | Soft Skill Coaching / Call Listening | Voice Coach |
| **Repeat Issue** | -60 Points | Process Adherence Audit | Quality Coach |
| **Long Hold** | -20 Points | Technical Refresher / SME Support | SME |
| **Wrong Fix** | -80 Points | Knowledge Assessment | SME / TL |

---

## TENURITY ANALYSIS
*   **0-3 Months:** High technical errors, long hold times.
*   **3-12 Months:** Best performance, balanced AHT/NPS.
*   **12+ Months:** Behavioral fatigue, "Scripted" sounding, lower NPS due to lack of empathy.
