# FILE: 11_Solution_Design_NPS.pptx

## SLIDE 1: PROJECT VISION
**Title:** Improving NPS via Time & Motion Study
**Goal:** Shift from "Process Adherence" to "Customer Experience".
**Target:** 15% -> 30% NPS.

---

## SLIDE 2: TIME & MOTION INSIGHTS
*   **Discovery:** 46% of time spent on diagnostics, but 15% is unproductive hold.
*   **Constraint:** Agents lack technical autonomy for new DSL profiles.
*   **Solution:** Empower agents with "SME Lite" training and real-time floor support.

---

## SLIDE 3: THE ERROR REDUCTION MODEL
*   **Process Errors:** Fixed via CRM auto-pop (remove repetition).
*   **Behavioral Errors:** Fixed via "Empathy Bridge" coaching.
*   **Knowledge Errors:** Fixed via SME-led "Deep Dive" sessions.

---

## SLIDE 4: THE COACHING FRAMEWORK
*   **Phase 1 (Diagnosis):** Daily detractor analysis led by Rahul Nethikar.
*   **Phase 2 (Correction):** Voice/Quality coaches target specific gaps.
*   **Phase 3 (Validation):** SMEs confirm technical accuracy of fixes.

---
