## KEY INSIGHTS FROM DETRACTOR/PASSIVE ANALYSIS
1.  **Passive Calls (NPS 7-8):** Agents are technically correct but "Mechanical". No wow factor.
2.  **Detractor Calls (NPS 0-6):** Hold times > 120s and repeat explanations are the #1 triggers.
3.  **Hold Time:** 60% of hold time is spent waiting for SME validation or searching the internal knowledge base.
4.  **Repetition:** 45% of calls involve the agent asking for information already provided in the IVR.
