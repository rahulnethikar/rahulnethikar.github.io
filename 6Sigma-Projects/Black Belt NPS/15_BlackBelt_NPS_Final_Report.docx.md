## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This project was initiated to address the critical gap in Customer NPS for the Verizon DSL technical support queue at Mahindra Satyam BPO. By leveraging a granular Time & Motion Study and statistical regression modeling, the project identified that technical resolution (FCR) and communication empathy are the primary drivers of customer advocacy. Through a center-wide implementation of the "Empathy Bridge" coaching framework and technical "SME Lite" empowerment, the center achieved its goal of shifting NPS from 15% to ~28%.

---

## DMAIC SUMMARY

**1. DEFINE:** Identified NPS at ~15% (Target: 30%). Stakeholders aligned under Rahul Nethikar's execution leadership.
**2. MEASURE:** Conducted Time & Motion Study of 120 calls. Baseline data confirmed high repeat calls (32%) and empathy gaps in 40% of detractor calls.
**3. ANALYZE:** Regression modeling confirmed Repeat Call Rate as the strongest negative predictor of NPS (Coeff: -5.5). Hold time was identified as a major source of customer frustration.
**4. IMPROVE:** 
*   Implemented Daily Detractor Reviews.
*   Deployed Voice Coach for empathy-based communication training.
*   Standardized the Repeat Call Prevention SOP.
*   Empowered agents with SME-lite knowledge to reduce hold times.
**5. CONTROL:** Established a multi-layer governance model (TL -> AM -> OM). Created an NPS Dashboard for weekly trend tracking.

## FINAL RESULTS (POST-IMPLEMENTATION)

## PROJECT BENEFITS
*   **Customer Loyalty:** Improved NPS leads to higher retention and lower churn for Verizon.
*   **Cost Efficiency:** Reduced repeat calls and lower AHT saved approximately 850 man-hours per month.
*   **Center Stability:** Standardized coaching model improved morale and reduced attrition in the bottom quartile.
