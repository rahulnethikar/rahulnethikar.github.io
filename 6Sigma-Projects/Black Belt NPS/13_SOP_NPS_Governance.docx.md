## STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP): NPS GOVERNANCE

**1. DETRACTOR HANDLING PROCESS**
*   **Step 1:** Every morning, the AM extracts the previous day's NPS survey results.
*   **Step 2:** All detractors (scores 0-6) are flagged for immediate RCA.
*   **Step 3:** The TL/SME listens to the full call and identifies the primary error (Process, Tech, Behavioral).
*   **Step 4:** A callback is initiated to the customer (where permitted) to offer resolution and "Close the Loop".
*   **Step 5:** Coaching feedback is documented in the central portal.

**2. COACHING ESCALATION MATRIX**
*   **Case A (Technical Error):** Immediate briefing by SME. If repeated, mandatory knowledge assessment.
*   **Case B (Behavioral Gap):** Voice Coach intervention. Use roleplay to correct empathy triggers.
*   **Case C (Process Defiance):** Quality Coach review and TL disciplinary warning.

**3. REPEAT CALL PREVENTION PROTOCOL**
*   **Protocol:** Every call must conclude with "Is there anything else I can help you with today?".
*   **Protocol:** Agent must summarize the fix and provide a ticket reference number.
*   **Protocol:** WFM to flag "High Repeat" ANI (Automatic Number Identification) for priority routing to SMEs.
