## 5-WHY ANALYSIS: LOW NPS

| Why Level | Root Cause Chain 1 (Behavioral) | Root Cause Chain 2 (Process) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Why 1?** | Customers giving low NPS scores | Customers complaining about hold time |
| **Why 2?** | They feel the agent is "Robotic" | Agents put calls on hold frequently |
| **Why 3?** | Agent follows script too strictly | Agent doesn't know the tech fix |
| **Why 4?** | Emphasis on AHT leads to rushing | Training was basic and outdated |
| **Why 5 (RC)** | **Lack of empathy coaching** | **Technical knowledge gap in new DSL profiles** |

---

## FISHBONE DIAGRAM FINDINGS (Ishikawa)

**1. People:**
*   High attrition leading to new-hire mix.
*   "AHT Pressure" vs "Resolution Focus".

**2. Process:**
*   Redundant authentication steps.
*   Weak detractor callback mechanism.

**3. Measurement:**
*   QA focus on compliance over customer experience.
*   NPS survey not discussed in coaching.

**4. Technology:**
*   Slow CRM response times.
*   Lack of real-time diagnostic tools for agents.

---

## CRITICAL RCA FINDINGS SUMMARY
1.  **Poor Empathy:** Agents miss emotional cues in 40% of detractor calls.
2.  **Repetition:** AHT increases by 45-60s due to repeating info from IVR.
3.  **Incorrect Resolution:** 12% of calls are "fixed" incorrectly, leading to guaranteed detractors.
4.  **Weak Closure:** Agents don't set expectations for the survey, leading to "surprised" passives.
