## CRITICAL TO QUALITY (CTQ) PARAMETERS

| CTQ Category | CTQ Requirement | Primary KPI | Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer Satisfaction** | Positive brand recommendation | **NPS** | 25 - 30% |
| **Resolution Quality** | Issue fixed in first attempt | **FCR** | > 75% |
| **Operational Efficiency** | Balanced call duration | **AHT** | 6.2 Minutes |
| **Service Consistency** | Adherence to process | **QA Score** | > 90% |
| **Customer Loyalty** | Low call recurrence | **Repeat Call Rate** | < 22% |
| **Agent Skill** | High resolution capability | **SME Validation** | > 95% |

---

## KPI DEFINITIONS
*   **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** % Promoters (9-10) minus % Detractors (0-6).
*   **CSAT:** Average score from 1-5 post-call surveys.
*   **AHT (Average Handle Time):** (Talk Time + Hold Time + Wrap Time) / Total Calls.
*   **FCR (First Call Resolution):** % of calls resolved without a callback in 48 hours.
*   **Repeat Call Rate:** % of customers calling back within 7 days for the same issue.
*   **QA Score:** Weighted average of compliance and soft skills from audit forms.
