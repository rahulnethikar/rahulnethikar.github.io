## DATA COLLECTION PLAN

| Data Element | Source | Frequency | Sample Size | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NPS Survey Scores** | Client VOC Portal | Daily | 100% of surveys | AM / OM |
| **QA Audit Scores** | NICE / Verint | Weekly | 5 calls per agent | Quality Control |
| **Call Recordings** | NICE Recording System | Ad-hoc | As needed for RCA | Quality Coach |
| **AHT / FCR Data** | CMS / Avaya Reports | Daily | Center-wide | WFM |
| **Repeat Call Logs** | CRM (Siebel/SAP) | Weekly | Center-wide | SME / TL |
| **Time & Motion Logs** | Manual Observations | Once (Measure) | 120 calls per shift type | Rahul Nethikar + 3 SMEs |

## TIME & MOTION STUDY SAMPLING STRATEGY
*   **Total Samples:** 120 calls.
*   **Stratification:**
    *   **Short Calls (< 3 mins):** 40 samples.
    *   **Medium Calls (3-7 mins):** 40 samples.
    *   **Long Calls (> 7 mins):** 40 samples.
*   **Shift Coverage:** 40 samples each for Early, Mid, and Graveyard shifts.
*   **Day Coverage:** 80 Weekday samples, 40 Weekend samples.
*   **Deep Dive:** 100% of Detractor and Passive calls from the last 30 days.
