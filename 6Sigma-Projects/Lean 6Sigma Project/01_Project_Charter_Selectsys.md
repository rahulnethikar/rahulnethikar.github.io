# PROJECT CHARTER
**Project Title:** Improving CSAT, Agent Utilization, and Quality in Premium Tech Support Operations
**Organization:** Selectsys India
**Client:** US Tech Support (Premium/Paid Services)
**Timeline:** February 2012 – May 2012

---

## 1. BUSINESS CASE
US Tech Support provides premium, paid technical assistance. Customers paying for support have high expectations for service quality and resolution speed. Currently, the operation is suffering from low customer satisfaction (CSAT) and poor agent productivity. Failure to improve will lead to high churn rates, loss of revenue, and brand damage for the client.

## 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
Between November 2011 and January 2012, the project recorded a CSAT score of ~75%, significantly below the target for a paid service. The customer experience is hindered by poor English communication in Chat and a broken transition from Chat to Voice/LMI. Furthermore, agent utilization is extremely low at ~30%, and reach rates for callbacks are at ~30%, indicating significant leakage in the resolution funnel and poor operational discipline.

## 3. GOAL STATEMENT
*   **CSAT:** Increase from 75% to 95% by May 2012.
*   **Utilization:** Improve from 30% to 90%.
*   **Quality:** Enhance QA scores and resolution quality through standardized communication and technical protocols.

## 4. PROJECT SCOPE
*   **In-Scope:** Chat Support (L1), Voice Support (L2), Remote Troubleshooting (LogMeIn/LMI), and Operational Governance.
*   **Out-of-Scope:** Billing disputes (handled by US Billing Team), HR Payroll processing.

*   **Revenue:** Improved utilization reduces the cost per resolution.
*   **Experience:** Professionalized communication restores brand trust.
