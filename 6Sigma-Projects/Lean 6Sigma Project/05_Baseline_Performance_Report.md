# BASELINE PERFORMANCE REPORT
**Reporting Period:** February 2012 (Start of Project)

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Initial assessment shows a critical gap between current performance and client expectations. The primary concerns are low customer satisfaction and extreme under-utilization of the workforce.

## 2. METRIC SUMMARY
| **Metric** | **Baseline Value** | **Target** | **Status** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CSAT** | 75.4% | 95.0% | 🔴 Critical |
| **Agent Utilization** | 32.1% | 90.0% | 🔴 Critical |
| **Reach Rate** | 30.5% | 75.0% | 🔴 Critical |
| **Average QA Score** | 78.0% | 90.0% | 🟡 Warning |
| **Unplanned Absenteeism**| 12.0% | <3.0% | 🔴 Critical |

## 3. KEY OBSERVATIONS
*   **CSAT:** High volume of "1-star" ratings specifically following LMI sessions that required a computer restart.
*   **Utilization:** Agents spend significant time in "Manual Wrap" or "Aux" mode without operational justification.
*   **Reach Rate:** No tracking mechanism currently exists for callbacks; agents make a single attempt and abandon the lead.
*   **Discipline:** Shift start times are not strictly enforced, leading to a "cascading" delay in floor coverage.
