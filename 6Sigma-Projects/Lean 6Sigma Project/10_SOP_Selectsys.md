**Organization:** Selectsys India
**Effective Date:** May 2012

## 1. CHAT COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES
*   **Greeting:** "Hello [Customer Name], thank you for contacting US Tech Support. My name is [Agent Name]. How may I assist you today?"
*   **Professionalism:** Use the `Approved_Responses.xlsx` library for all technical explanations.
*   **Closing:** "Is there anything else I can assist you with, [Customer Name]?"

## 2. RESTART & CALLBACK PROTOCOL (MANDATORY)
1.  Obtain customer consent for a system restart.
2.  **Disclaimer:** "I will now initiate a restart. The remote session will disconnect. I will call you back on [Phone Number] in 2 minutes to continue the fix."
3.  Initiate Restart.
4.  Dial customer immediately using the Cudatel VOIP dialer.
5.  Reconnect via LogMeIn (LMI).
6.  Verify Resolution.

## 3. VOICE CALLBACK HANDLING
*   Attempt 1: Immediate post-disconnect.
*   Attempt 2: 15 minutes post-disconnect (if Attempt 1 fails).
*   Attempt 3: 1 hour post-disconnect (if Attempt 2 fails).
*   Log all attempts in the `Reach_Tracker`.

## 4. FLOOR DISCIPLINE RULES
*   Shift login is at **exactly** 00:00 HH:MM per roster.
*   Break entitlement: 15 min Tea, 30 min Lunch.
*   No unplanned leaves; all leaves must be logged in the HR portal 24 hours in advance.
