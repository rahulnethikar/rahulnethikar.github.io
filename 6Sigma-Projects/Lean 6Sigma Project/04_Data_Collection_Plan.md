# DATA COLLECTION PLAN
**Project:** Improving CSAT & Utilization - US Tech Support

---

| **Data Element** | **Source** | **Frequency** | **Responsible** | **Collection Method** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CSAT Surveys** | CRM Export | Daily | Rahul Nethikar | Automated survey triggers exported to CSV. |
| **QA Audits** | Quality Scorecards | Weekly | Quality Assurance (QA) | Random sampling of 5 chats/calls per agent. |
| **VOIP Utilization** | Cudatel Reports | Daily | Voice Team TL | Real-time status tracking from Cudatel PBX. |
| **Attendance** | Biometric System | Daily | Rahul Nethikar / HR | Punch-in/out data correlated with shift roster. |
| **Resolution Rate** | CRM Ticket Logs | Weekly | Rahul Nethikar | Analysis of repeat tickets for the same UID. |

---

**Objective:** To establish a data-driven baseline for the Measure Phase and monitor improvement trends in the Improve Phase.
