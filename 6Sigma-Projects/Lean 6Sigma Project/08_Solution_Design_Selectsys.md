# SOLUTION DESIGN: OPERATIONS TRANSFORMATION
**Project:** Improving CSAT & Utilization - US Tech Support

---

## 1. BEFORE VS. AFTER COMPARISON

### **Old Journey (Fragmented)**
*   **Chat:** Casual English, no name addressing.
*   **Restart:** Session breaks → Customer gets survey → Customer angry → Resolution fails.
*   **Productivity:** 30% Util. Agents hide in "Aux" mode.

### **New Journey (Standardized)**
*   **Chat:** Professional, template-driven English.
*   **Restart:** Clear verbal/chat disclaimer → Planned disconnect → Immediate callback → Resolution verified → Manual Survey trigger.
*   **Productivity:** 90% Util. Real-time monitoring of Cudatel status.

## 2. PRODUCTIVITY IMPROVEMENT MODEL
*   **The 5-Minute Rule:** Agents have maximum 5 minutes in "Manual Wrap" mode before the system auto-switches them to "Available."
*   **Roster Alignment:** Breaks are staggered to ensure 100% floor coverage during US peak hours (9 AM - 1 PM EST).

## 3. EXECUTION LEADERSHIP (RAHUL NETHIKAR)
*   **Bridge Role:** Acts as the enforcer of the OM's strategy.
*   **Daily Governance:** 10 AM Daily Stand-up with Chat AM and Voice TL to review yesterday's CSAT and Utilization.
*   **Direct Coaching:** Floor-walking during peak hours to ensure "Restart Protocol" adherence.
