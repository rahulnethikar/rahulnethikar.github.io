# CTQ & KPI DEFINITIONS
**Project:** Improving CSAT & Utilization - US Tech Support

---

| **Critical to Quality (CTQ)** | **KPI Name** | **Operational Definition** | **Baseline** | **Target** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer Satisfaction** | CSAT | % of customers rating service 4 or 5 out of 5 in the post-session survey. | ~75% | 95% |
| **Resource Efficiency** | Utilization | (Total Logged-in Time - Total Break/Aux Time) / Total Shift Time. | ~30% | 90% |
| **Process Compliance** | QA Score | Adherence to English communication standards, empathy, and technical protocols. | Low | 90% |
| **Customer Reach** | Reach Rate | % of successful outbound callbacks to customers for L2/LMI sessions. | ~30% | >70% |
| **Problem Resolution** | Resolution Rate | % of issues fixed without a repeat contact in 48 hours. | N/A | 85% |
