## SLIDE 2: Improvement Strategy Overview
*   **The 4 Pillars of Success:**
    1.  **Communication Clarity:** Managed by Voice Coach.
    2.  **Process Integrity:** Managed by Quality Coach.
    3.  **Technical Accuracy:** Managed by SME.
    4.  **Operational Discipline:** Managed by Team Leader.

## SLIDE 3: Coaching & Governance Model (MANDATORY INTEGRATION)
*   **Graphic Description:** A centralized "Command Center" (TL) supported by 3 specialist modules.
*   **Voice Coach:**
    *   *Focus:* Communication clarity, reducing wordiness/repetition.
    *   *Impact:* Decreases AHT by 45-60 seconds.
*   **Quality Coach:**
    *   *Focus:* Process adherence, correct call flow mapping.
    *   *Impact:* Prevents rework and "Right First Time" compliance.
*   **SME:**
    *   *Focus:* Deep-dive technical troubleshooting & Callback authorization.
    *   *Impact:* Reduces Repeat Call Rate and Outbound Utilization.
*   **Team Leader (TL):**
    *   *Focus:* Real-time floor management, escalation handling.
    *   *Impact:* Directly drives Service Level and Wait Time adherence.

## SLIDE 4: New Call Flow Architecture
*   **Step 1:** Call Inbound -> Initial Diagnostic.
*   **Step 2:** If complexity score > 3 -> **Mandatory SME Intervention**.
*   **Step 3:** Post-call -> **Voice/Quality Coach Sampling** (High-Risk Agents).
*   **Step 4:** Callback Required -> **TL Approval Loop**.

## SLIDE 5: Expected Outcomes (Post-Implementation)
*   **Service Level:** 92% (Up from 78%)
*   **AHT:** 6.1 min (Down from 7.4 min)
*   **FCR:** 90% (Up from 78%)
*   **Outbound Util:** 8% (Down from 18%)
