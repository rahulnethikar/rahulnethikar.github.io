## 🎖️ Featured Six Sigma Projects

### 🌑 [Six Sigma Black Belt: Service Level & Wait Time Optimization](file:///C:/Users/shiva/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/Projects/6Sigma%20Black%20Belt%20Service%20Level/15_BlackBelt_Final_Report.docx.md)
**Client:** Verizon DSL Technical Support (Mahindra Satyam BPO)
**Timeline:** 2010 | **Scope:** Center-wide Implementation

A comprehensive project addressing critical Service Level failures through a multi-layered coaching and governance framework.

*   **Primary Problem:** SL < 85%, High AHT (7.4m), and excessive Outbound utilization.
*   **Key Achievement:** Improved Service Level from **78% to 92%** and reduced Wait Time by **60%**.
*   **Methodology:** Full statistical layer with Hypothesis Testing and Regression ($SL = f(AHT, OB\%, Repeat\%)$).
*   **Innovation:** Integrated the specialist role model (Voice Coach, Quality Coach, SME, and TL) into real-time production flow.

### 🟢 [Six Sigma Green Belt: Outbound Reduction & Callback Governance](file:///C:/Users/shiva/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/Projects/6Sigma%20Green%20Belt/README.md)
**Client:** Verizon DSL Technical Support (Mahindra Satyam BPO)
**Timeline:** 2009 | **Scope:** Workgroup-level Implementation

Focused on optimizing counselor availability by curbing unnecessary outbound callbacks and implementing the "NLAP" (No Lines Actively Pending) framework.

*   **Primary Problem:** High Outbound callback volume draining inbound capacity.
*   **Key Achievement:** Reduced Outbound utilization through structured MLT-based callback validation.
*   **Legacy:** Established the foundational callback governance used in subsequent center-wide Black Belt expansions.
