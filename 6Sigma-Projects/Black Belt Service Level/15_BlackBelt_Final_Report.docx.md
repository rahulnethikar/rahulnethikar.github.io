## FINAL PROJECT REPORT: SIX SIGMA BLACK BELT PROJECT
**Project Name:** Service Level & Wait Time Optimization
**Organization:** Mahindra Satyam BPO
**Client:** Verizon DSL Technical Support
**Project Lead:** Rahul Nethikar (Team Leader / Execution Lead)

---

## 1. PROJECT SUMMARY
Between Q1 2010 and Q3 2010, this Black Belt project addressed a critical Service Level (SL) performance gap in the Verizon DSL Technical Support center. By implementing a multi-layered coaching and governance framework and optimizing high-impact variables (AHT and Repeat Calls), the project successfully exceeded all target KPIs, stabilizing the center's performance and improving customer experience.

## 2. RESULTS AT A GLANCE

| Metric | Baseline | Post-Improvement | % Change |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Service Level (SL)** | 78% | 92% | +18% |
| **Average Wait Time** | 45s | 18s | -60% |
| **Average Handle Time** | 7.4m | 6.1m | -17.5% |
| **Repeat Call Rate** | 22% | 11% | -50% |
| **Outbound Utilization**| 18% | 8% | -55% |
| **First Call Resolution**| 78% | 90% | +15% |

## 3. STATISTICAL VALIDATION
*   **Hypothesis Testing:** Confirmed with p-values < 0.05 that Voice Coaching and SME Interventions were the primary drivers of AHT and Repeat Call reduction.
*   **Regression Model:** Demonstrated that Service Level is highly sensitive to AHT and Outbound utilization (R-Sq = 86%).
*   **Control Charts:** Validated that the process has reached a new state of stability with narrow control limits and a significantly improved mean for all core metrics.

## 4. COACHING-DRIVEN PROCESS IMPROVEMENT (EXPLANATION)
The primary catalyst for success was the structural integration of specialized roles across the production floor:

1.  **Voice Coach (Communication Optimization):** By focusing on reducing verbal "clutter" and repetitive instructional sequences, the Voice Coach reduced AHT by an average of 78 seconds per call. This directly increased center-wide availability.
2.  **Quality Coach (Process Adherence):** The Quality Coach ensured that agents followed the specific "Value-Added" call flow, preventing the need for callbacks or rework caused by incomplete initial diagnostics.
3.  **SME (Technical Accuracy & Governance):** The Subject Matter Expert acted as a technical "firewall," validating complex cases in real-time. This reduced Repeat Call Rates by 50% and ensured that Outbound callbacks were only utilized when technically necessary.
4.  **Team Leader (Operational Discipline):** The TL provided the governance substrate, managing floor occupancy and real-time SLA adherence, ensuring that the improvements designed by the coaches and SMEs were executed consistently.

## 5. FINANCIAL IMPACT & ROI
*   **Capacity Gain:** Improved efficiency (AHT reduction) resulted in a 15% increase in call-taking capacity without additional headcount.
*   **Estimated Annual Savings:** $120,000 (Based on staffing optimization and reduced penalty payouts for SL misses).
*   **Final ROI:** 800%.
