## SIPOC DIAGRAM: Center-wide DSL Support Optimization

| Supplier | Input | Process | Output | Customer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verizon Client** | Technical Knowledge Base (KB), Systems Access | **1. Call Arrival:** WFM routes call to available agent | Service Level (SL) Metrics | Verizon Customers |
| **HR / Training** | Skilled Manpower, Voice Training | **2. Troubleshooting:** SME provides technical guidance for complex cases | Resolved Customer Issue | Verizon Client |
| **Voice Coach** | Communication Guidelines | **3. Quality Check:** Quality Coach audits calls | Compliance Scores | Operations Manager (Owner) |
| **Quality Coach** | Process SOPs | **4. Coaching:** Voice Coach addresses repetition | Reduced AHT | Project Sponsor / Owner |
| **SME** | Technical Expertise | **5. Floor Governance:** TL manages escalations | Improved Wait Time | General Manager (Sponsor) |
| **WFM** | Staffing Schedules | **6. Callback Governance:** SME/TL validate necessity | Low Outbound Util | Rahul Nethikar (Execution Lead) |

---

## ROLE INTEGRATION IN PROCESS

1.  **SME:** Acts as the primary supplier of technical accuracy during step 2 (Troubleshooting).
2.  **Voice Coach:** Provides inputs to step 4 (Coaching) to minimize AHT by reducing filler words and repetitive explanations.
3.  **Quality Coach:** Inputs to step 3 (Quality Check) to ensure "Right First Time" (RFT) delivery.
4.  **TL:** Manages the entire flow (Floor Governance) to ensure adherence to SLA.
