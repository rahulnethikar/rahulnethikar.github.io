## CTQ & KPI DEFINITION

| Customer Requirement (CTQ) | Metric (KPI) | Unit | Baseline (Current) | Target (Goal) | Role Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accessibility** | Service Level (SL) | % | 78% | ≥ 90% | **TL/WFM:** Scheduling & Floor control |
| **Speed to Answer** | Average Wait Time | Sec | 45 | < 20 | **TL:** Real-time floor management |
| **Effective Resolution** | First Call Resolution (FCR) | % | 78% | > 88% | **SME:** Technical accuracy check |
| **Minimize Effort** | Repeat Call Rate | % | 22% | < 12% | **Quality Coach:** Process adherence |
| **Efficiency** | Average Handle Time (AHT) | Min | 7.4 | ≤ 6.2 | **Voice Coach:** Communication clarity |
| **Availability** | Outbound Utilization | % | 18% | < 10% | **TL/SME:** Callback governance |
| **Connection Quality** | Call Drop Rate | % | 7% | < 5% | **WFM:** Occupancy management |

---

## OPERATIONAL DEFINITIONS

*   **FCR (First Call Resolution):** A call where the customer does not call back for the same issue within 72 hours.
*   **Repeat Call Rate:** Total calls involving the same account/issue within 72 hours divided by total calls.
*   **AHT (Average Handle Time):** [Talk Time + Hold Time + Wrap Time] / Total Calls.
*   **Service Level:** Percentage of calls answered within 90 seconds.
