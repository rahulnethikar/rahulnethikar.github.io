## SLIDE 1: The NLAP Standard (Jaydeep's Workgroup)
We are not just cutting outbound calls; we are improving how we resolve technical issues on our floor.
*   **The PIN System:** Not a barrier, but a check-point to ensure we've done all we can.
*   **The MLT Commitment:** We still call our customers back for MLT results.

## SLIDE 2: Coaching for MLT Callbacks
*   **The Problem:** Agents are rushing MLT calls to avoid "Dead Air."
*   **The Training:** Coach agents on multitasking during an MLT run.
*   **The Rule:** If a callback is offered for MLT, the agent must set a specific time and document the MLT Transaction ID.

---

## SLIDE 3: Quality Standards for Outbound
*   A "Good" Outbound call has an MLT or NOC reason.
*   A "Bad" Outbound call has a "Follow-up" or "General" reason with no technical notes.
*   We coach on the **difference** between a valid technical requirement and a tactical outbound.

---

## SLIDE 4: Building the "Resolution First" Culture
*   No more parking calls.
*   No more lazy callbacks.
*   Focus on the technical data (Modem codes, Line Sync, MLT).
