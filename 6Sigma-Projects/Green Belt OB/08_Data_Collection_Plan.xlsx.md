---

## Operational Definitions
*   **Outbound Call:** Any non-inbound call initiated by an agent from their workstation.
*   **PIN Approval:** A unique code entered by an SME to unlock the outbound dialing capability.
*   **Mandatory Troubleshooting:** Completion of all "Physical Layer" checks before escalating for a callback.
