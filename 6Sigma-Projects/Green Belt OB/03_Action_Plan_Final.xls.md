## SOLUTION DESIGN RATIONALE
*   **Poka-Yoke:** The PIN system acts as an "Error Proofing" mechanism to prevent emotional or tactical outbound dialing.
*   **Skillset Change:** Moving follow-ups to a dedicated post-shift queue (controlled by WFM) ensures that agents on the floor stay available for fresh inbound calls.
