# CTQ AND KPI DEFINITION - JAYDEEP MISTRY'S WORKGROUP

| Metric (KPI) | Definition | Business Impact | Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Outbound %** | (Total Outbound Calls / Total Inbound) * 100 | Higher outbound reduces **Jaydeep's Floor** capacity. | **<= 15%** |
| **AHT** | Average duration of a technical interaction. | Directly affects workgroup staffing cost. | **< 600 Sec** |
| **First Call Resolution** | % of customers resolved on first contact. | Primary driver of workgroup CSAT. | **> 75%** |
| **CSAT (Customer Satisfaction)** | Score based on post-call surveys (Top Box %). | Reflects brand health and churn probability. | **> 85%** |
| **SME Escalation %** | Percentage of calls requiring SME intervention for PIN or troubleshooting. | Measures agent proficiency and process adherence. | **< 10%** |

---

## Critical to Quality (CTQ) Attributes
*   **Speed to Resolution:** Minimized AHT without compromising technical accuracy.
*   **Process Compliance:** Adherence to the PIN-based outbound restriction workflow.
*   **Technical Accuracy:** Correct diagnosis of "No Sync" vs "Authentication" issues.
